The school year is approaching and distance-learning has become the new normal during this crazy and unprecedented time. Many of you may feel overwhelmed with the daunting task of homeschooling your children and I am here to help! I have experience teaching children ages 4-12 and would love to help homeschool or tutor your children.

After graduating college from the University of Arizona, I moved to Houston, Texas where I taught fifth grade. Back in San Diego, I pursued a career in early childhood education where I taught Pre-K and Kindergarten for 6 years. I wanted to gain more experience with older children, so for the past two years I have been substitute teaching at inner-city schools.

I have worked within a wide demographic of children and understand how important it is to build a trusting relationship with them. I am capable of modifying my instruction towards the needs of your child and promise to make this an enriching and fun experience for them.
